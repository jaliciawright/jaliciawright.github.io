About

Originally from Georgia, Alicia Wright holds a B.A. from Middlebury College. She moved to Brooklyn and has worked as a bookseller, a literary scout, and as part of a publishing startup. She has attended the Bread Loaf Writers' Conference and has been nominated to submit work to Best New Poets 2014. She is pursuing an MFA at the Iowa Writers' Workshop.
