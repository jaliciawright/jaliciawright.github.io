About

Originally from Georgia, Alicia Wright holds a B.A. from Middlebury College. She lived in Brooklyn and worked at the Strand, as a literary scout, and as part of a publishing startup. She has attended the Bread Loaf Writers' Conference and has been awarded fellowships at the Iowa Writers' Workshop, where she recently earned her MFA. Presently, she's an adjunct assistant professor at the University of Iowa.
