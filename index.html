About

Alicia Wright is originally from Rome, Georgia. She holds a B.A. from Middlebury College; she lived in Brooklyn and worked at the Strand, as a literary scout, and as part of a publishing startup. She has attended the Bread Loaf Writers' Conference and has been awarded fellowships at the Iowa Writers' Workshop, where she recently earned her MFA. Presently, she's an adjunct assistant professor at the University of Iowa. This fall, she will begin a PhD in Creative Writing and Literature at the University of Denver.
