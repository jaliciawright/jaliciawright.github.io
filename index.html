About

Alicia Wright is from Rome, Georgia. She attended Middlebury College; she lived in Brooklyn and worked at the Strand, as a literary scout, and as part of a publishing startup. She's been awarded a scholarship from the Bread Loaf Writers' Conference and fellowships from the Iowa Writers' Workshop. At present, she's a PhD candidate in English & Literary Arts at the University of Denver, and serves as Poetry Editor for Denver Quarterly.
