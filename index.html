About

Originally from Georgia, Alicia Wright holds a B.A. from Middlebury College. She lived in Brooklyn and worked as a bookseller, a literary scout, and as part of a publishing startup. She has attended the Bread Loaf Writers' Conference and has been awarded fellowships at the Iowa Writers' Workshop, where she recently earned her MFA.
